About Us

  SaveLife is a life saving portal designed by students to help people in India during pandemic. Our main motto is to save millions of life by bringing all necessary requirements in a single platform. Patients can find doctors, necessary tool kits during pandemic like masks, sanitizers, oxygen concentrators, important tips to save lives. It is a complete end-to-end portal to help people to stand strong.
